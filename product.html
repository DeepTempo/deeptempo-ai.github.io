DeepTempo: Revolutionizing Cybersecurity with Advanced Deep Learning

Enhancing threat detection with fine-tuning and MITRE mapping integration.

Discover how DeepTempo's innovative solution, leveraging the Snowflake Native App Framework, accelerates threat detection and response times for enhanced cybersecurity.

DeepTempo has unveiled new capabilities for Tempo, its cutting-edge cybersecurity solution powered by deep learning. Now available on the Snowflake Marketplace, Tempo offers improved threat detection accuracy and faster response times. By integrating fine-tuning and MITRE mapping, the solution provides security teams with enriched data and insights directly within their existing SIEM systems.

"This ensures that security teams can continue leveraging their SIEM while benefiting from the enhanced intelligence provided by DeepTempo," said Evan Powell, founder and CEO. The solution operates seamlessly within existing workflows, allowing organizations to gain improved protection without the need for significant changes to their infrastructure.

Prasanna Krishnan of Snowflake highlighted the innovation Tempo represents: "By bringing deep learning directly into their Snowflake accounts, customers can limit costly data movement and dramatically reduce time to value."

Tempo's MITRE ATT&CK flagged alerts integrate effortlessly into existing SIEM platforms, reducing mean time to respond (MTTR) and saving valuable time during active threats. The model uses network and cloud flow logs to identify common attack types, tagging sequences with MITRE ATT&CK techniques for efficient analysis.
