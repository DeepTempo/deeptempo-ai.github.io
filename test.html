DeepTempo Enhances Cybersecurity with Fine-Tuning and MITRE Mapping

Deep learning-powered solution boosts threat detection accuracy and cuts response times, showcasing the strength of the Snowflake Native App Framework.

DeepTempo introduces advanced capabilities for its cybersecurity solution, Tempo, enhancing threat detection accuracy and reducing response times. Leveraging the Snowflake Native App Framework, Tempo integrates seamlessly into existing SIEM systems, mapping detected anomalies to MITRE ATT&CK sequences, and providing actionable insights.

Enhancements in Cybersecurity with DeepTempo

Tempo's new fine-tuning capabilities allow organizations to customize models to their environment, ensuring precise threat detection. Operating upstream from a customer's SIEM, Tempo enriches data, flowing insights directly into current workflows. This integration, powered by Snowflake Cortex AI, offers improved protection efficiently and cost-effectively.

Snowflake Native App Framework and Deep Learning

By integrating deep learning capabilities within Snowflake accounts, DeepTempo minimizes data movement and enhances time to value. Security teams can react swiftly with MITRE ATT&CK flagged alerts streaming into SIEM, reducing MTTR during active threats.
