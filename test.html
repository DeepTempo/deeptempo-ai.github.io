DeepTempo Enhances Cybersecurity with Fine-Tuning and MITRE Mapping

Deep learning-powered solution boosts threat detection accuracy and cuts response times, showcasing the strength of the Snowflake Native App Framework.

Discover how DeepTempo's innovative cybersecurity solution integrates with existing systems to enhance protection and operational efficiency.

DeepTempo today announced exciting new capabilities for Tempo, its deep learning-powered cybersecurity solution on the Snowflake Marketplace. With advanced fine-tuning, MITRE mapping integration, and seamless compatibility with existing SIEM systems, Tempo offers enhanced context and actionable insights for security teams.

“This ensures that security teams can continue leveraging their SIEM while benefiting from the enhanced intelligence provided by DeepTempo,” said Evan Powell, founder and CEO, DeepTempo. Leveraging the Snowflake Native App Framework and Snowflake Cortex AI, DeepTempo delivers improved protection quickly and cost-effectively.

Tempo's fine-tuning capabilities allow organizations to adapt models to their specific environments, ensuring greater accuracy in threat detection. Users benefit from enhanced protection and threat isolation, paying directly from their Snowflake account. Tempo operates within their environment, seamlessly flowing enriched data into existing workflows.

Prasanna Krishnan, head of collaboration and horizon at Snowflake, notes, “DeepTempo's rapid progress in deploying advanced deep learning solutions is the innovation we envisioned with the Snowflake Native App Framework.” By bringing capabilities directly to customers' Snowflake accounts, data movement is minimized, and time to value is significantly reduced.

Security teams with predefined response plans can now react with unprecedented speed using Tempo’s MITRE ATT&CK flagged alerts, which integrate seamlessly into SIEM platforms. This integration reduces mean time to respond (MTTR) and has proven to save critical time during active threats.

Using only network and cloud flow logs, Tempo identifies common attacks such as reconnaissance, lateral movement, and data exfiltration. It automatically tags sequences with the closest MITRE ATT&CK techniques, embedding vital information in compact representations for efficient analytics and reduced log storage costs.

DeepTempo provides deep-learning-based cybersecurity solutions that protect enterprises from cyber attacks, available on the Snowflake Native App Marketplace and for on-premise deployment. To learn more, visit DeepTempo's website or follow them on LinkedIn, YouTube, and Medium.
