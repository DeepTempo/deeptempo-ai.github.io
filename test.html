DeepTempo Enhances Cybersecurity with Fine-Tuning and MITRE Mapping

Deep learning-powered solution boosts threat detection accuracy and cuts response times, showcasing the strength of the Snowflake Native App Framework

DeepTempo has announced new capabilities for Tempo, a deep learning-powered cybersecurity solution available on the Snowflake Marketplace. With enhanced fine-tuning and MITRE mapping integration, Tempo provides enhanced context and actionable insights while maintaining compatibility with existing SIEM systems.

Enhanced Threat Detection with Tempo

DeepTempo's Tempo operates upstream from existing SIEM systems, allowing security teams to leverage enriched data and insights seamlessly within their workflows. Through the Snowflake Native App Framework and Snowflake Cortex AI, users receive improved protection in a quicker and more cost-effective manner.

The fine-tuning capabilities of Tempo enable organizations to adapt models to their specific environments, ensuring greater accuracy in threat detection. Security teams can trigger responses swiftly with MITRE ATT&CK flagged alerts, significantly reducing mean time to respond (MTTR).

Tempo's model uses network and cloud flow logs to identify common cyber attacks, automatically tagging sequences with MITRE ATT&CK techniques. The compact embeddings, being less than 1% the size of original logs, improve analytics speed and reduce storage costs.
