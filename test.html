DeepTempo Enhances Cybersecurity with Fine-Tuning and MITRE Mapping

Deep learning-powered solution boosts threat detection accuracy and cuts response times, showcasing the strength of the Snowflake Native App Framework.

DeepTempo has introduced new capabilities for Tempo, its cutting-edge cybersecurity solution available on the Snowflake Marketplace. With improved fine-tuning, MITRE mapping integration, and seamless compatibility with existing SIEM systems, Tempo enhances threat detection and response efficiency.

“This ensures that security teams can continue leveraging their SIEM while benefiting from the enhanced intelligence provided by DeepTempo. Through the Snowflake Native App Framework and the capabilities of Snowflake Cortex AI, we can deliver improved protection to our users in a quicker and more cost-effective way,” stated Evan Powell, founder and CEO of DeepTempo.

Tempo’s fine-tuning capabilities enable organizations to tailor models to their environments, increasing accuracy in threat detection. The solution operates upstream from a customer’s existing SIEM, integrating enriched data seamlessly into current workflows. Users pay from their Snowflake account, ensuring Tempo runs within their environment.

Prasanna Krishnan, head of collaboration and horizon at Snowflake, remarked, “The progress of DeepTempo in deploying advanced deep learning solutions for cybersecurity embodies the innovation we envisioned with the Snowflake Native App Framework.”

Security teams can respond to cyber attacks with unprecedented speed using Tempo's MITRE ATT&CK flagged alerts. This integration significantly reduces mean time to respond (MTTR), saving critical time during active threats.

Using network and cloud flow logs, Tempo identifies threats like reconnaissance and data exfiltration. It tags sequences with MITRE ATT&CK techniques, embedding this information in compact representations to enhance analytics efficiency and reduce log storage costs.
