DeepTempo Enhances Cybersecurity with Fine-Tuning and MITRE Mapping

Deep learning-powered solution boosts threat detection accuracy and cuts response times, showcasing the strength of the Snowflake Native App Framework.

DeepTempo has introduced new capabilities for its deep learning-powered cybersecurity solution, Tempo, available as a Snowflake Native App. With advanced fine-tuning, MITRE mapping integration, and seamless SIEM compatibility, Tempo enhances threat detection and response efficiency.

Tempo operates upstream from existing SIEM systems, enriching data flow and maintaining workflow continuity. This innovative solution, leveraging Snowflake Cortex AI, enhances security team capabilities by providing actionable insights and improved protection in a cost-effective manner.

The fine-tuning feature allows organizations to customize models to their specific environments, ensuring accurate threat detection. Security teams can trigger precise responses with MITRE ATT&CK flagged alerts, significantly reducing mean time to respond (MTTR).

Tempo's use of network and cloud flow logs allows for the identification of various attacks, including reconnaissance and data exfiltration. By embedding information in compact representations, Tempo enhances analytics efficiency and reduces log storage costs.

DeepTempo continues to innovate in deploying deep-learning-based cybersecurity solutions, available on the Snowflake Native App Marketplace. To learn more, visit DeepTempo's official website or explore their LinkedIn page, YouTube channel, and Medium posts.
