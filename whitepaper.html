Abstract

In today's rapidly evolving cybersecurity landscape, traditional rule-based and machine learning approaches are struggling to keep pace with sophisticated threats. DeepTempo introduces a revolutionary approach that leverages deep learning models specifically designed for log analysis.

Our LogLM technology understands the semantic meaning and context within log data, enabling it to detect anomalies and potential threats that other solutions miss. This whitepaper details the architecture of our system, presents performance benchmarks against leading ML/UEBA solutions, and provides case studies demonstrating real-world applications.

The paper also explores how DeepTempo's Snowflake Native App brings these capabilities directly to where your data resides, eliminating the need for complex data movement and enabling real-time threat detection at scale.
