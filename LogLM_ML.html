Introduction

Traditional machine learning approaches in cybersecurity lack the agility to adapt to rapidly evolving threats. ML based cybersecurity systems tie together a mix of models, and humans are required to select features within the models and then to select the models themselves. As a result, traditional approaches take a long time to deliver value and are brittle, unable to quickly adapt to changes in your environment.
