DeepMitre: From NetFlow to MITRE ATT&CK

Harnessing our Tempo LogLM and advanced classifiers to map security logs to MITRE ATT&CK techniques.

How Does It Work?

We host the Tempo LogLM alongside a classifier that leverages high-dimensional embeddings of logs. These embeddings, condensed representations of raw logs, are compared to the most similar MITRE ATT&CK techniques, enabling rapid threat mapping.

Limitations

Each user can perform only a handful of comparisons today. Please get in touch if you'd like to run Tempo yourself or try it out on Snowflake—where it’s available as a NativeApp with a free tier.

Why Not Open Source the Foundation Models?

Security

We work with large enterprises and strategic partners. Open sourcing the foundation models might inadvertently assist sophisticated attackers.

Competition

While there is high interest in our LogLM foundation models, our current strategy is to keep them closed, opening adjacent pieces of code to foster innovation.
