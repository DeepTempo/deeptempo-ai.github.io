DeepTempo Enhances Cybersecurity with Fine-Tuning and MITRE Mapping

Discover how DeepTempo boosts threat detection accuracy with deep learning and Snowflake integration.

DeepTempo, a leading cybersecurity solution provider, has announced exciting new capabilities for its Tempo solution, now available as a Snowflake Native App. With advanced fine-tuning, MITRE mapping integration, and compatibility with existing SIEM systems, security teams can now map detected anomalies to MITRE ATT&CK sequences, enhancing context and insights. The solution also allows for seamless operation within existing workflows, improving protection and reducing costs.

DeepTempo has unveiled significant updates to its deep learning-powered cybersecurity solution, Tempo. Designed as a Snowflake Native App, Tempo now enhances threat detection and response capabilities with fine-tuning and MITRE mapping. This boosts the accuracy of threat detection while reducing response times, showcasing the power of the Snowflake Native App Framework.

Seamless Integration with Existing Systems

Evan Powell, founder and CEO of DeepTempo, highlights that Tempo integrates effortlessly with existing SIEM systems. This allows security teams to utilize their current setups while gaining enhanced intelligence from Tempo. Powered by Snowflake Cortex AI, the solution offers improved protection at reduced costs.

Advanced Fine-Tuning Capabilities

Tempo’s fine-tuning allows organizations to adapt the solution to their specific needs, ensuring accurate threat detection. Operating upstream from a customer’s SIEM, enriched data flows into existing workflows, enhancing efficiency without additional data movement.

Speed and Precision in Threat Response

With MITRE ATT&CK flagged alerts, security teams can execute response plans with unmatched speed and precision. This integration significantly reduces mean time to respond (MTTR), saving critical minutes or hours during active threats.

Efficient Data Handling

Using network and cloud flow logs, Tempo identifies common cyber threats, tagging sequences with MITRE ATT&CK techniques. Compact data representations, or embeddings, ensure faster analytics and reduced costs.
