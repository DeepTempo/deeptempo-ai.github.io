Cybersecurity powered by Deep Learning

Detect threats with unprecedented accuracy and adaptability and then rapidly understand and isolate them. DeepTempo's Tempo LogLM is a purpose-built deep learning model that understands the language of logs, improving threat detection, isolation, and remediation.

Collective Deep Learning For Cybersecurity

DeepTempo builds LogLMs (Log Language Models), Foundation Models uniquely able to rapidly adapt to new environments while retaining extraordinary accuracy. The LogLMs are used to better identify potential attacks.

DeepTempo helps to keep defenders ahead of attackers by increasing the productivity of cyber defense while saving costs by applying our Tempo LogLM-based solutions.

Deep learning is more than cutting-edge technology. Foundation models, like Tempo LogLM, can apply what they learn in one environment to each new environment they encounter. They are collective intelligence - at work in defense of us all.

Mission

DeepTempo delivers Collective Defense via Deep Learning. We return the power to defenders with LogLMs and software. We take open approaches, running on any data lake, and reducing lock-in from aging vertically integrated security platforms.

Where Do We Fit In The Enterprise?

Tempo runs in your Datalake where your data lives. It can even run as a Snowflake Native App. Using Tempo will save you money on your SIEM, by sending incidents, not raw logs, into your SIEM.
